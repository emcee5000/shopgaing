Consumer Reports Product Info

Insignia™ - 28" Class (27-1/2" Diag.) - LED - 720p - 60Hz - HDTV

Model: NS-28E200NA14 - SKU: 7376049

With 720p resolution and a 30,000:1 dynamic contrast ratio, this Insignia™ NS-28E200NA14 LED HDTV lets you watch your favorite TV shows and movies in crisp high-definition. Two 5W speakers deliver immersive audio.

Connect a Roku Streaming Stick to this HDTV and get access to more than 700 channels, including Netflix, Hulu Plus and HBO GO.

Product Features

27-1/2" screen measured diagonally from corner to corner For optimal viewing in medium-size rooms. Roku Ready Pair with Roku Streaming Stick for access to more than 700 channels, including Netflix, Hulu Plus and HBO GO. 3D Y/C digital comb filter Provides enhanced visuals. A 6.5 ms response time helps eliminate blurring and juddering to ensure smooth delivery of fast-motion content. The 178° horizontal and vertical angles enable customizable viewing. 30,000:1 dynamic contrast ratio (3000:1 typical) Offers crisp, detailed images. 220 cd/m² brightness For a highly visible display. 720p display Provides an excellent viewing experience. DTS Sound technology Enhances audio playback, providing clear, balanced and natural sound with deep, enveloping bass. Inputs Include 1 HDMI, 1 HDMI/DVI shared, 1 component video, 1 composite/component shared, 1 PC, 1 component/composite audio shared and one 3.5mm PC/DVI audio. Outputs Include 1 headphone/analog audio and 1 coaxial digital audio. 2 HDMI inputs HDMI cable not included. High-speed HDMI cable is the only connection that can deliver a full HDTV experience with a full 1080p picture and digital surround sound. DVI interface Provides a direct digital-to-digital video hookup with compatible devices. PC video input Lets you connect your computer to experience high-resolution images. USB port Supports JPEG file formats, so you can enjoy viewing your photos on the big screen. Also supports firmware upgrades. MHL (Mobile High Definition Link) enabled Supports 1080p HD video and 5.1-channel audio surround sound playback from your mobile devices (requires MHL enabled phone or tablet and MHL cable, not included). Useful additional features Include a sleep timer and channel labeling.
